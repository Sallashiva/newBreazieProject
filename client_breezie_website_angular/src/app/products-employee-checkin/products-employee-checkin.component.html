Employee check in

The app will help the employees to feed in their in and out time in the office premises. That will help the organization to keep a check on the timings and attendance of the employees.

Remote working is made easy

The world has now become comfortable with the fact that there will be times when the employees will have to work remotely and the work can still be managed in that manner. If the employees are asked to use this app, they can keep the organization informed that they are working remotely. This will also help the organization to know the whereabouts of their employees.

Keeping a record of employee sign in

This app provides you an on site sign in, this will help you to know who is present in the premises and who is not. This makes the tracking and evacuation management easy. The employees can also sign in using the QR code provided.

Mobile Check in

Employees will have to download the Breazie app in order to use contactless sign in. All they have to do is answer a few questions related to the sign in protocol of the company. And their presence will be marked.

Roaming employees management

There are some employees in every office who have to travel to multiple branches of that particular company in order to manage the work. With the help of Breazie you can easily track these employees as well.

Health and Safety measures

While signing in the app also asks questions related to employees health. This is beneficial for the organization as with this information they can provide better help to that particular employee and also protect other employees from catching the infections.

In and Out board for employees

The employees can just use the tablet as an in and out board or can download the app in order to mark their presence in the office premises.
