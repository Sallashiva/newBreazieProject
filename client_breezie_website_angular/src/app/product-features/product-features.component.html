Zero Contact Sign in

Whenever any visitors enter the office, all they have to do is take their phone, open the camera, scan the QR and a form will pop up asking for the information that is required by the organization. The content of the form can differ from organization to organization as it is customisable.

Screening Visitor and Employee -

The visitors will be asked to fill a few questions on the app, which will give us data about their current health condition. This will help the organization to protect the workplace from any infectious diseases.

Evacuation Management -

This app comes to help, when you need to have a roll call on urgent basis and you do not have time to print the data first and then follow the process. You can just pick up the ipad and check , it will give you the entire data of employees present.

Digital Agreement -

Breazie helps the visitors to feed in their information that is required by the organization. This also helps the organization to create a database of people without any paperwork involved.

Instant Notifications -

This feature will give you an update that your visitor has arrived by sending you a message stating the name and the details of the visitor that is waiting for you.

ID badge printing -

Giving an official access to the visitors is very important not just for security purposes but also because this helps the visitors feel welcomed. As soon as the visitor will feed in their details an ID will automatically be generated that can be printed and handed over to the visitor. This will be an access card for him/her.
